A Nose for News

Pat was a journalist at heart and had a thurst for finding the truth. Pitter was not afraid to ask tough questions no matter who he was interviewing. In 2013 Pat teamed up with Sahbu founder Mitch Weight to help with devestating situtation for children in Liberia. They embarked on a trip to Liberia to attempt to bring to light the many issues including 80% of school age girls have turned to prostituion, and 66% of girls 10-19 have been raped.

In memory of Patrick and his love for journalism his parents established a scholarship for an outstanding student in journaism at Weber State University where Pat graduated in 1998. You can donate to the scholarship to help keep the memory of Pitter alive and helping young journaists succeed.
